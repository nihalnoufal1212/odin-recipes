Biriyani

Description

Biriyani is a flavorful rice dish made with spices, meat, and rice.

It is one of the most popular dishes in India and loved for its rich taste.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Spices
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash and prepare rice.
  2. Cook chicken with spices.
  3. Cook rice separately.
  4. Layer rice and chicken together.
  5. Cook for a few more minutes.
  6. Serve hot.

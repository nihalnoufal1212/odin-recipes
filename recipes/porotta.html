Porotta

Description

Porotta is a flaky layered flatbread popular in Kerala.

It is commonly eaten with beef curry, chicken curry, or vegetable curry.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water, salt, and oil into dough.
  2. Let the dough rest.
  3. Roll and stretch the dough.
  4. Fold into layers.
  5. Cook on a pan until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot.

Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a layered pasta dish made with meat, cheese, sauce, and pasta sheets.

It is a popular Italian dish loved for its rich flavor and cheesy texture.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook ground beef and mix with sauce.
  3. Prepare cheese mixture with eggs and seasonings.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese mixture.
  5. Bake for about 45 minutes.
  6. Let cool slightly and serve.
